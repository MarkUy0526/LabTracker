What kind of date range picker?

The chart X-axis will show each day in the selected range. Pick the style that fits best.

A — Two date inputs (From / To)

Simple From and To date fields inline with the title. Can span any range — days, weeks, or months. Defaults to the current full month on load.

Pros

  • Maximum flexibility
  • No extra library
  • Native browser UI

Cons

  • Can span across many months, making X-axis crowded

B — Month picker + day range

A month dropdown plus start/end day number inputs. Range is always within one month. X-axis stays clean and predictable (max 31 points).

Pros

  • X-axis never gets crowded
  • Clear "within a month" framing

Cons

  • Can't span across month boundaries

C — Prev / Next month arrows

Always shows a full month. Navigate with arrow buttons. Defaults to current month. Next arrow is disabled for future months.

Pros

  • Cleanest UI — one click to navigate
  • Always a full month view

Cons

  • No sub-month range selection
