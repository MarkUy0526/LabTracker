What should each line in the chart represent?

The chart will show days of the selected month on the X-axis. We need to decide what data each line tracks.

A

One line per equipment

Each piece of equipment gets its own colored line. Day 1 might show: Microscope = 3, Centrifuge = 1, etc. Good for spotting which equipment is in demand on specific days.

X: 1, 2, 3 ... 30 (days)
Lines: Microscope, Centrifuge, Pipette, ...
B

Single line — total borrows per day

One line showing the total number of borrow requests across all equipment per day. Cleaner and easier to read — great for spotting overall lab activity trends.

X: 1, 2, 3 ... 30 (days)
Line: Total requests (all equipment combined)
